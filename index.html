Aeneas

Αἰνείας . Aineías . Эней . /aɪˈniːəs/

This is a space where I share my experiences, learnings, research, and projects.

Why do I think these topics deserve a website and your time? Because I passionately use my skills to leverage technology as a tool for a safer, fairer, and healthier world. I have proven so by founding, developing and steering, in countless voluntary hours, the largest non-profit E-Learning Movement in Europe, Serlo Education. Now I am the founder of ORY, a company building and maintaining popular Open Source Projects. My vision is to make the web a safer and more accessible place.

This space is dedicated to my projects, and the learnings I made. I think they will be useful to you, especially if you are, like me, a visionairy developer - or aspiring to become one. I've hidden my creative works here, too. You might find them, if you look close enough.

Feel free to contact me on Twitter, GitHub and LinkedIn, any time.
